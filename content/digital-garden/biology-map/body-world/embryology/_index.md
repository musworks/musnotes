## Overview

This region examines the beginning of life and the complex transformations from a single cell to a multicellular organism. It focuses on the precise stages of cleavage, gastrulation, and organogenesis.

## Purpose

This node acts as a detailed repository for developmental biology. It is designed to document structural identifiers during differentiation and the temporal logic of embryonic growth with high precision.
