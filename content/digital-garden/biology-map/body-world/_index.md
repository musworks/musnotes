## Overview

This region focuses on the organismal level of biological existence. It explores how life takes physical form, develops through time, and functions as a complex, integrated system.

## Purpose

This node serves as a central hub for studying life as a macroscopic entity. It provides a structured space to document morphology, physiological processes, and the developmental stages that define different lineages of life.
