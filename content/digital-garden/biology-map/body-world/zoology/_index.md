## Overview

This domain explores the animal kingdom, focusing on the biological principles that govern animal life, from anatomical structures to behavioral patterns.

## Purpose

This node serves as a systematic archive for zoological data. It provides a structured environment to document faunal diversity, physiological systems, and the evolutionary logic of animal lineages.
