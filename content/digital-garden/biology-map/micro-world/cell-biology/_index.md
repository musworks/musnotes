## Overview

This section explores the fundamental unit of life. It focuses on the intricate architecture of cells, the organelles that drive their function, and the physiological processes that maintain cellular homeostasis.

## Purpose

This node serves as a repository for detailed observations and theoretical knowledge regarding cellular mechanisms. It is designed to document structural identifiers, metabolic pathways, and the essential logic of biological building blocks.
