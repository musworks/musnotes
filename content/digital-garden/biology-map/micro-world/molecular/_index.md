## Overview

This domain focuses on the molecular underpinnings of life, examining the interactions between various systems of a cell, including the relationship between DNA, RNA, and protein synthesis.

## Purpose

This node acts as a systematic archive for molecular theory and data. It documents the logic of genetic expression, biochemical pathways, and the precision required to understand biological phenomena at the most granular level.
