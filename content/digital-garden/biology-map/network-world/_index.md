## Overview

This region explores the interconnectedness of life. It examines how organisms relate to one another and their environment, forming complex networks of interaction, evolution, and shared history.

## Purpose

This node serves as a hub for macroscopic and relational biology. It organizes knowledge regarding biodiversity, ecological patterns, and the overarching systems that govern the distribution of life on Earth.
