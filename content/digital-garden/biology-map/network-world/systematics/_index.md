## Overview

Systematics studies how living organisms are named, grouped, and related. It provides the conceptual map that makes biological diversity readable and comparable across contexts.

## Purpose

This node acts as a navigational hub for taxonomy-oriented notes, connecting naming conventions, classification logic, and broader biological structure.
