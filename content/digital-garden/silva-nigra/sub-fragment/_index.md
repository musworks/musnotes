## Overview

A fragment of a fragment. This is the home for "shards" and "particles"—single sentences, fleeting images, or isolated words that carry weight but lack a broader context.

## Purpose

This node functions as a microscopic archive for atomic thoughts. It ensures that even the smallest "splinter" of an idea is preserved before it can be integrated into a larger structure.
