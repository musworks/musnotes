## Overview

This region evokes a darker and more associative terrain, where thought moves through shadow, resonance, memory, and imaginative relation rather than strict sequence. It is the "black forest" of the mind where ideas grow wild.

## Purpose

This node functions as a space for exploratory and associative thinking. It allows fragments, intuitions, and symbolic patterns to coexist without requiring immediate closure or formal order.
