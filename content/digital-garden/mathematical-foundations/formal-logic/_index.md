## Overview

Formal logic is the study of the principles of valid inference and demonstration. This section explores symbolic languages, propositional logic, and the structural laws of thought that remain consistent across different domains of knowledge.

## Purpose

This space functions as a lab for analytical reasoning. It documents the rules of deduction and induction, providing the tools to dissect complex arguments and build sound intellectual models.
