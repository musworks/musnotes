## Overview

Set theory is the fundamental language of modern mathematics. It deals with the properties of collections of objects—whether they are numbers, biological species, or abstract concepts—and the relationships between them.

## Purpose

This node organizes the principles of membership, Venn diagrams, and relational structures. It is particularly useful for understanding classification systems and the boundaries between distinct categories of information.
