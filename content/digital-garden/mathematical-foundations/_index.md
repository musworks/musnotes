## Overview

This domain focuses on the underlying architecture of mathematical thought. Rather than numerical calculation, this space is dedicated to the study of formal systems, abstract structures, and the basic rules that govern logical reasoning.

## Purpose

This node serves as a conceptual anchor for understanding the "grammar" of mathematics. It provides a stable place to explore how logic, sets, and foundational theories provide the rigorous framework necessary for science, philosophy, and systematic thinking.
